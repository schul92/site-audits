Our Story

Proudly Serving NYC Since 1988

What began as KC Flowers has grown into TJ Flowers & Events, a premier floral studio on the Upper East Side. For nearly four decades, we've been crafting beautiful designs of the finest quality for New Yorkers.

We have extensive experience with weddings, charity galas, corporate events, and lobby maintenance for NYC's most prestigious venues — including the Four Seasons, St. Regis, and the Plaza Hotel.

As NYC's trusted orchid specialist, we carry over 66 varieties of orchids, each hand-selected for health and beauty. Every arrangement leaves our studio with the same care and attention we'd give our own family.
